Acquiring a Talent
Your hero's talent was determined when he was first created, but it remains hidden, and thus provides no bonus, until he attains the of Deputy Chief. Because you cannot directly select your hero's talent, if you don't discover the talent you hoped for, you will have to create a new hero and rank him up again. Each hero will discover that he has one of the three talents available to his class. Courage is a unique talent that any class can have, but none will ever discover it as their hidden talent; it is only available on heroes.

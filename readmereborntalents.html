Acquiring a Talent
Talents are extra customization perks that you may select when creating new officers. Unlike Traits and Specializations, each talent can only be used by a subset of the classes, with each class getting 3 talents to choose from (with the exception of Courage, which all officers can select). Talents can be ranked up like other attributes. One talent is unlocked for every 75 achievement points earned - the talent unlocked is entirely random, except Courage will always be unlocked first. Talents are unlocked at a rank that is based on your renown level.

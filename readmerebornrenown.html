Renown
When the game ends, your performance can increase your officer's Rank and Renown experience.

Renown is not attached to any specific build you make, but rather rather your Steam account. It has a cap at level 55 and levels up slower than Rank (especially for levels 51-55). Certain renown levels unlock perks that become available on all builds immediately. Renown level is also used to calculate your votekick weight and strength of the -revive command.
